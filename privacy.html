Fuel — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-30

Fuel — iRacing Calculator is a local calculator app for sim racing fuel and stint planning.

Data collection

The app does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

Local storage

The app may store calculator inputs, presets, and session values locally on your device so the calculator can restore your previous state. This data stays on your device and is not sent to a server by the app.

Tracking and analytics

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, use the project support page or GitHub repository linked from the app listing.
