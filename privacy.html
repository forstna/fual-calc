Fuel Calc — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-05

Fuel Calc is a local calculator app for sim racing fuel and stint planning.

Data collection

Fuel Calc does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

Local storage

All calculations are performed locally on your device. The app does not operate an account system, backend service, or user database.

Tracking and analytics

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, use the support page linked from the App Store listing.
