Fuel — iRacing Calculator

Fuel helps sim racers estimate race fuel, stint strategy, pit stops, safety margin, and live fuel projections.

Help

If a calculation looks wrong, check lap time format, fuel-per-lap, tank size, and safety margin first. For time-based races, use a realistic average lap time including traffic.

Contact

Support is handled through the GitHub project or the support URL listed in the App Store.
